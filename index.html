Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's everything you need to know about finding love with DesireYourLove.

How does your matching system work?

We use a combination of your preferences, interests, values, and conversation patterns to suggest compatible matches. Our algorithm learns from your interactions to improve suggestions over time, focusing on long-term compatibility rather than just physical attraction. We also consider factors like life goals, communication style, and relationship readiness.

Is DesireYourLove free to use?

Yes! You can create a profile, browse matches, and start conversations completely free. We also offer premium features like advanced filters, unlimited likes, read receipts, and priority support for those who want to supercharge their dating experience. Premium starts at just $19.99/month.

How do you ensure user safety and authenticity?

Safety is our top priority. All profiles are manually reviewed within 24 hours, we offer photo verification, secure in-app messaging, and easy reporting tools. We use AI to detect suspicious behavior and have a dedicated safety team available 24/7. We also provide comprehensive dating safety tips and never share your personal information with other users.

What makes you different from other dating websites?

We prioritize quality over quantity. Instead of endless swiping, we encourage meaningful conversations through thoughtful icebreakers and conversation starters. Our community guidelines promote respect and genuine connections. We also offer relationship coaching, date planning assistance, and follow-up support to help relationships flourish beyond the first date.

Can I use the app if I'm over 50?

Absolutely! Love has no age limit, and our community includes people from 18 to 80+. We have specific features designed for mature daters, including life-stage matching, and many of our success stories involve people who found love later in life. Age is just a number when it comes to finding your perfect match.

Do you support LGBTQ+ relationships?

Yes! We're proud to be an inclusive platform that welcomes people of all sexual orientations and gender identities. Our matching system includes comprehensive options for gender identity and sexual preference, and we have specific community guidelines to ensure a safe, respectful environment for everyone.

Can I delete my account anytime?

Absolutely! You have full control over your account and data. You can pause your profile, delete it temporarily, or permanently remove all your information from our platform at any time through your settings. We also offer a "success story" option if you've found love and want to share your experience!

How long does it typically take to find a match?

While every journey is unique, most of our users start having meaningful conversations within the first week. About 60% of our users go on their first date within a month, and the average time to find a serious relationship is 3-6 months. Remember, we focus on quality matches, not quantity, so patience often leads to better outcomes.
